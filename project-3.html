Project 3

GAN-Based Paintings Generator with Advanced Discriminator Design and Training Techniques

Live Link

Project Overview

  • Paintings Generator using GANs
  • Developed and implemented GAN models, including a U-Net-based discriminator and a U-Net decoder-based discriminator, to generate high-quality paintings.
  • Trained models using CutMix augmentation and consistency regularization techniques to enhance feature learning and improve the generator's output.
  • Evaluated and compared the performance of different models across various datasets to ensure robustness and generalization.

Achievements

The project advanced the application of GANs in creative domains, contributing to the generation of realistic and diverse paintings, and offered insights into improving GAN training techniques.
