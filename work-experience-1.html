Project Overview

During my Data Science internship at BNP Paribas in Paris (Jul 2023 – Jan 2024), I developed a machine learning pipeline for Biodiversity Intactness Index (BII) estimation. My work involved testing various regression models, including Linear Regression, CatBoost, and RandomForest, to predict species abundance and regional similarity. Additionally, I constructed a housing unit dataset and implemented machine learning models (Linear Regression, XGBoost, RandomForest) to predict energy performance diagnosis (DPE) based on unit features.

By the end of the internship, I published a research paper based on my work, where I have presented all the indices that could be used for biodiversity monitoring. You can access the paper by clicking the button below.
